Cells are updated automatically when another cell is selected. 
Here are the ways that cells can be selected in the spreadsheet:

1-Click on the cell.
2-Arrow keys will move the cell selection in the direction of the arrow key.
3-Enter will move the selection down the spreadsheet.
4-Shift + Enter will move the selection up the spreadsheet.
5-Tab will move the selection right on the spreadsheet.
6-Shift + Tab will move the selection left on the spreadsheet.
